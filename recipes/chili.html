Instant Pot Chili

A quick and easy recipe for delicious chili!

This recipe uses the instant pot to make hot, hearty chili without having to toil over a hot stove or wait all day for a slow cooker. With a few ingredients and the help of the instant pot, you can have a warm, filling bowl of comforting chili in under an hour, with very little prep!

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1 1/2 Pounds of Ground Beef
  • 6 Strips of Bacon, chopped
  • 1 15 oz. Can of Kidney beans, drained
  • 1 15 oz. Can of Pinto beans, drained
  • 1 15 oz. Can of Black beans, drained
  • 1 15 oz. Can of Fire Roasted Diced Tomatoes with juice
  • 1 6 oz. Can of Tomato Paste
  • 1 large Red Onion, chopped
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, seeded & chopped
  • 1 Jalapeno pepper, seeded & minced *optional*
  • 2 cups Beef Stock
  • 1 Tablespoon Dried Oregano
  • 2 Teaspoons Ground Cumin
  • 2 Teaspoons Kosher Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • 2 Tablespoons Chili Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Minced Garlic

For Garnish:

  • Sour Cream
  • Cilantro
  • Shredded Cheese

Directions:

  1. Set Instant Pot to "Saute" and add the bacon.
  2. Cook until crisp, stirring often.
  3. Remove the bacon to a paper towel lined plate, leaving Instant Pot on Saute.
  4. Add the onions and peppers and cook until tender.
  5. Add the Ground Beef and cook until browned.
  6. Drain off any excess grease by tilting the inner pot, holding back the meat with a large spoon or ladle.
  7. Add all remaining ingredients and 3/4 of the bacon, stir to combine.
  8. Close and lock the lid of the Instant Pot, make sure the vent is set to "sealed."
  9. Set the Instant Pot to "Manual," high pressure, and cook for 18-20 minutes.
  10. Allow pressure to release naturally for 10-15 minutes, then quick release the pressure by turning the vent to "venting."
  11. Serve with limes, sour cream, shredded cheese, and top with the remaining bacon!
